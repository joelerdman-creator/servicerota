# Graphic Generation Standards

**Style Version**: v1.0

## Master Style Anchor
`warm authentic community photography aesthetic, soft natural lighting, inclusive and diverse subjects including families with children, muted earth tones with gold accents, ultra-realistic candid editorial photography, photorealistic, 16:9 cinematic panoramic aspect ratio, wide landscape orientation, focus centrally on church volunteers joyfully serving in their scheduled roles, architecturally correct layout with pews and chairs always explicitly facing forward toward the front stage or altar, dynamic authentic interactions between volunteers and members, no text overlays, consistent depth of field`

## Rules
1. **Always Append**: ALL image generation prompts MUST have the Master Style Anchor appended to the end of the prompt to ensure consistent branding and visual cohesion.
2. **No Freestyling**: Agents must *never* freestyle or dynamically adjust style parameters outside of the bounds of the anchor and registry fragments. Stick to the anchor + registry combination.
3. **Applies Everywhere**: This rule applies universally to any workflow or tool that generates images for the web application or promotional assets.
